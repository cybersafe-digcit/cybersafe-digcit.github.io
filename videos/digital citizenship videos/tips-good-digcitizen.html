Tips on how to be a good digital citizen!






QUESTIONS


1. What are some ways to be a good digital citizen? List 3 ways.


2. What should you NOT do when talking to people online?


3. Should you post whatever you want online? Why not?


4. What are some personal details you should NOT share?




SCROLL DOWN TO CHECK YOUR ANSWERS

















ANSWERS


1. Treat others as you’d like to be treated; Don’t feed the trolls;
Don’t take what doesn’t belong to you; Think carefully about what you post;
Be careful with personal information; Don’t believe everything you see online.


2. You should not insult, bully, or start arguments with people.


3. You should always be careful and not post anything that could hurt you
if anyone finds it years later.


4. Phone number, mailing address, credit card information,
home address, driver’s license number.
